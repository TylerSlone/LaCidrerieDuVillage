Welcome to La Cidrerie Du Village!

Treat yourself to a sweet escape at our cider house. Discover the Cidre du Verger and our cidre Tranquille, ciders without bubbles. Taste Joie d'Automne and Montérégien, two traditional method sparkling wines, not to mention our Folie de glace, and above all, come and try our Coup de Foudre, an ice cider aperitif.

Take the opportunity to stock up on fresh apples, natural and sparkling juice, pies, jellies, marinades, vinegar, etc. A warm welcome awaits you.
